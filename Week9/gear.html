Black Diamond Camalot #1

This was my first piece of trad gear, so it's my favorite. A good trusty no. 1. Fits in so many places, is fairly light, and the prettiest color (imho). I love this piece of gear in a totally normal and not parasocial way.

Black Diamond ATC Guide

An excellent alpine belay device. ATC Guides are super cool because you can make them auto-blocking if belaying from above, which is so useful on long alpine climbs where you don't want the extra weight and complexity of a GriGri.

Black Diamond Camalots #4 and #5

These beefy boys are just fun. Not particularly useful in most places (they're too big to place in a lot of cracks). But damn, if they don't look cool and make you feel awesome when you can use them.
