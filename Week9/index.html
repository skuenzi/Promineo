After Six, Yosemite Valley

This was such a fun climb, but so much harder than expected. The first pitch, which has a polished crack system, is supposed to be 5.7. Hardest 5.7 I've ever climbed. I took two newbies up this climb and one of them cried. Which is understandable. But now she's totally into climbing so it's fine.

Olive Oil, Red Rocks Canyon

I've climbed this twice. The first time was horrifying (climbing with an inexperienced follower in the dark, running out of rope, the climb itself really run-out). The second time was really fun. It's got great exposure and some really fun moves.

Southeast Arete, Baboquivari Peak

This was QUITE the adventure. It was incredibly fun, but exhausting. The hike down in the dark was difficult, to put it nicely. But overall, a really cool experience.
